Stuffed Peppers with Rice and Sauce

Total time

60 min

Level

Easy

Diners

4

Use small colored peppers for this Halloween decoration!

Ingredients
  • 8 medium-sized colored bell peppers
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 400 grams of long-grain rice
  • 1 onion
  • 1 handful of raisins
  • A splash of soy sauce
  • Olive oil, Salt, Pepper and black olives
    • Instructions
  • Preheat oven to 200°C.
  • Prepare colored bell peppers: cut off tops, remove seeds, and carve eyes and mouth.
  • Place on a tray, drizzle with oil, season, and roast for 30 mins.
  • Cook rice with oil, water, and salt for 15-18 mins.
  • Chop red bell pepper and onion, sauté in oil.
  • Add raisins, rice, soy sauce, and mix well.
  • Fill peppers, garnish with olives, and top with lids.
